About Us

We are very glad in introducing ourselves as Sai NTV Associates, one of the renowned entities engaged in offering quality Acoustics & Interior Design services in Bangalore. We are Acoustic Consultants & Contractor, associated with SAI JYOTI BUILDERS Developers, Planners & Constructions that play a vital role in our success. Our wide sphere of services includes Acoustical services, Designing & Interior services, Engineering - civil work, Sound Engineering. The entire range of services is precisely engineered to all aspects of acoustics & interior design.

We at Sai NTV Associates believe in building and nurturing a lifelong relationship with our customers through providing a world class service experience of Acoustics, Interiors and Engineering.

Sai NTV Associates, which believes in the significance of client satisfaction, quality based management and culture of progressive development within the company, for a sustainability in the sector. Our adroit team holds expertise in resolving the challenges of multi-functional spaces in interiors and total acoustical solutions for buildings, theaters, multiplexes, industries and other noise vibration units. We are widely applauded in the market for their traditional and contemporary styles which make us a preferred choice among our esteemed clients. Our ultimate aim is to exceed the clients' expectations with our valued services. We work on certain parameters to offer the quality like Schematic Design, Design development and Material Specifications. As far as the schematic design are concerned we do Drawing analysis from the design view point, Noise, Vibration or Decoration parameters measurements and Segregation and development of CAD drafting.

We are highly experienced service company focused on developing custom, highly - quality solutions on - time and below budget for a variety of small, medium and large sized customer. With qualified and dedicated professionals on board, Sai NTV Associates undertakes turn-key projects. Over the last 12 years, our experienced engineers and staff have completed various reputed commercial, residential, official, public recreational centers, auditoriums etc., with extreme focus on quality while meeting budget and timeline targets. It is this dedication to quality and performance targets that has gained us the respect from our customers and clients.
